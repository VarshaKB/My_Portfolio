## üß© Featured Research Case Studies

### 1. Predictive Employee Attrition & Retention Intelligence  
**Domain:** Data Science √ó Predictive HR Analytics  

- Reframed attrition as a **time-to-event problem** instead of binary classification  
- Implemented **Survival Analysis (Cox Proportional Hazards)** to estimate attrition risk over time  
- Applied **Double Machine Learning (DML)** to isolate causal impact of managerial interventions  
- Integrated **Transformer-based NLP sentiment signals** from employee feedback  

**Impact:**  
- ~92% predictive accuracy  
- 14.6% simulated attrition reduction  
- Built on 38,000+ structured and unstructured data points  

---

### 2. AI-Driven Workforce Skill Graph & Internal Mobility Engine  
**Domain:** Graph Neural Networks √ó Strategic HR Planning  

- Designed a **Knowledge Graph** to model skill adjacency and latent talent  
- Embedded employee-role-skill relationships using **Sentence Transformers**  
- Applied **Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)** to predict role transition feasibility  
- Optimized internal mobility decisions at enterprise scale  

**Impact:**  
- 41% increase in internal role fulfillment  
- Reduced dependency on costly external hiring  
- Scaled to 22,000+ employee profiles
